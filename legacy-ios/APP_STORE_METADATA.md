## Description (4000 chars max)

Companies are profiting from your personal data every single day. Data brokers, ad networks, AI companies, and Big Tech platforms collect your name, address, location, browsing habits, health data, purchase history, and more -- often without your knowledge or consent.

The law says you can stop them. Revoke makes it easy.

WHAT REVOKE DOES

Revoke generates legally compliant data deletion emails based on your state's privacy law. Browse 178+ companies organized by category, preview the email, tap "Open in Mail," and hit Send. That's it.

Each email cites the specific statute that applies to you -- whether it's California's CCPA, Virginia's VCDPA, or any of the 17+ US state privacy laws currently in effect. Companies are legally required to respond within 45 days.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Choose a company or send to an entire category at once
2. Preview a pre-written legal deletion request
3. Tap "Open in Mail" -- your email app opens with everything pre-filled
4. Hit Send. Revoke saves a receipt and starts the countdown.
5. Track responses and follow up on overdue requests

FEATURES

- 178+ companies across 10 categories: Data Brokers, AI, Surveillance, Social Media, Ad Networks, Health, Finance, Retail, Search, and Location
- Legal emails that cite your state's specific privacy statute
- Deadline tracking with push notification reminders
- Attorney General complaint generator for companies that don't respond
- Full-text search across all companies
- "Send All" batch mode for entire categories
- Request types: Delete, Opt Out, or Access your data
- Portal detection for companies that require web forms
- Copy-to-clipboard fallback if Mail isn't configured

YOUR PRIVACY

Revoke collects absolutely nothing. Your name and email are stored only on your device. We have no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no tracking. We never see your emails.

We built this app because we believe in privacy. It would be hypocritical to violate yours.

WHY $0.99

Other data deletion services charge $100-$500 per year. Your privacy shouldn't be a luxury. Revoke runs entirely on your device with no servers to maintain. One dollar. That's it. Forever.
